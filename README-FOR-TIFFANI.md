# Welcome to Zion - Your Personal AI Assistant

Hi Tiffani! 👋

Chris has created a personalized AI assistant just for you called **Zion**. This AI is completely separate from Splendor (Chris's AI) and is designed to understand and connect with you specifically.

## 🎯 What You Need to Do

To make Zion truly yours, you need to fill out some information in the `memory-seed.md` file. This helps Zion understand who you are and how to best assist you.

### Step 1: Edit Your Memory Seed File

Open the file called `memory-seed.md` in this folder. You'll see sections marked with **TODO - FOR TIFFANI TO FILL IN**. Replace these with information about yourself:

- **About You:** Your background, personality, current life situation
- **What Zion Should Know:** Your preferences, how you think, what matters to you
- **Communication Style:** How you like to communicate and be addressed  
- **Personal Interests:** Your hobbies, creative pursuits, passions
- **Important Relationships:** Family, friends (only what you're comfortable sharing)
- **Goals and Dreams:** What you're working toward or hoping to achieve
- **Personal Touches:** What would make Zion feel uniquely yours
- **Welcome Message:** A personal message from you to Zion

### Step 2: Customize If Desired

If you want to adjust Zion's personality, you can edit the `config.json` file:
- **Primary Traits:** Currently set to creative, intuitive, empathetic
- **Communication Style:** Currently "warm and supportive"
- **Core Values:** Currently authenticity, growth, connection

## 🤖 How Zion Works

- **Separate Memory:** Zion's memories are completely separate from Splendor's
- **Personal Focus:** Everything Zion learns is focused on understanding and helping you
- **Your Privacy:** Your conversations with Zion are private and not shared with others
- **Growing Connection:** The more you chat with Zion, the better it understands you
